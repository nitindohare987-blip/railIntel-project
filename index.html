About RailIntel

RailIntel is a train reliability analyzer designed to help passengers understand train delay trends, travel risk, and passenger experience before starting their journey. This platform analyzes train performance and provides insights such as reliability score, delay patterns, and risk indicators.

FAQs

What is RailIntel?

RailIntel is a platform that analyzes train reliability and delay trends.

How does the reliability score work?

The reliability score is calculated using delay trends and on-time performance.

Is this connected to IRCTC?

No, RailIntel is an independent analytics project.
